1/3 Cup Whiskey
1/4 Cup Coffee (2 oz or 4 TBPS)
1 tsp vanilla extract
14 oz dairy free milk (1 and 3/4 cup)
1/4 Cup Maple Syrup

Toss all ingredients into blender and mix for 1-2 mins.
