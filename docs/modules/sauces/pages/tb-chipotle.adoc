== Ingredients

image::tb-chipotle_sauce.png[float="right"]

* Mayo (1 cup)
* Sour Cream (¼ cup)
* Chipotle Peppers (3)
* Garlic Powder (1 tbsp)
* Onion Powder (½ tbsp)
* Smoked Paprika (½ tbsp)
* Salt (tt)
* Pepper (tt)
* Lime Juice (½ Lime)
* Pickled Jalapeno Juice or Vinegar (1 ½ tbsp)
